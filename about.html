    Globus International entered the service industry in 2016 and made its mark in the segment through a combination of operational excellence, industry expertise, and transformation capabilities. company with many year of collective proven expertise in the field of industrial supplies. Globus International is presently serving across India, whilst our headquarters being in Indore, Madhya Pradesh.

    Globus International , Through their exclusive sourcing outlet have been successfully, sourcing and supplying reliable industrial products such a Safety PPE’s, Packaging material, Oil & chemical and industrial consumable to their client base.
